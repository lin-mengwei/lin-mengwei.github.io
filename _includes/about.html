My name is Mengwei Lin. I am a PhD candidate at the University of Barcelona (UB) School of Economics and the Barcelona Institute of Economics (IEB, Public Policies), where I work with Andreu Arenas and Jenifer Ruiz-Valenzuela. From Spring 2024 to 2026, I also attend the PhD Course Program in Economics of Education at the University of Zurich (Swiss Leading House VPET-ECON).

My interests mainly lie in the Economics of Education, as well as Public Economics and Labor and Demographic Economics; on the method side, I do causal inference, and am starting some structural adventures. I am also a certified translator and popularization article writer.

Prior to Barcelona, I worked at the China Development Institute (Research Center for Public Policy, with WANG Mei) and as a freelance translator, after obtaining my Master's Degree in Economics (with Distinction) from the SGPE Programme at The University of Edinburgh.

My first name "Mengwei" has two syllables, meng (pronunciation: IPA /m…ông/, audio here) and wei (pronounced as the word "way").
