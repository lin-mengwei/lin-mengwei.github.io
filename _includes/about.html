I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Barcelona (UB) and the Barcelona Institute of Economics (IEB), working with Andreu Arenas and Jenifer Ruiz-Valenzuela since 2023. I participated in the PhD Course Program in Economics of Education at the University of Zurich from 2024 to 2025.


Research fields:

  • Applied Microeconomics (topics: education, culture, labour)
  • Urban Economics
  • Causal Inference Methods

Complementing research, I initiate and organize activities targeting young researchers, and write public-facing econ articles.


Prior to Barcelona, I worked in policy consultancy at the China Development Institute (Public Policy Center) and was a book translator. I obtained my MSc in Economics (with Distinction) from The University of Edinburgh.


* My name "Mengwei" (孟蔚) has 2 parts: meng (like /məng/, audio) & wei (as "way").
