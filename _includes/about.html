My name is Mengwei Lin. I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Economics,School of Economics at the University of Barcelonaand a predoctoral researcher at theBarcelona Institute of Economics (IEB).Prior to Barcelona, I obtained my MSc in Economics (with Distinction) from the University of Edinburgh.

My research interests are in the Economics of Education, Labor Economics, Public Policy, and Causal Inference.

Besides, I work as a certified English-Chinese translator, focusing on book translation in the general field of Social Sciences. (Latest:《经济学怎么了》- What's Wrong with Economics? by Robert Skidelsky)

Please feel free to drop me an email if you would like to discuss research, translation, or any other topics (or simply to say hello).
